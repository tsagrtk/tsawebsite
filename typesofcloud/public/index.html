Types of Cloud- Public

Public

With Public Computing, the whole computing system lies with the company.

How it works:

They excel in performance, but they can be easily hacked. The way this is done is because public clouds use shared resources, hence the name, and that is why they are easily hacked, but because they share, they are very fast.

Benefits:

  • Utility pricing
  • Speed
  • Elastic resource capability
  • Virtualized resources
  • Management automation
  • Self-service provisioning
  • Third-Party ownership
  • Managed Operations
